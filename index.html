    How to read this

    Plain-English definitions for everything in Edge Intelligence and Stats Snapshot. The math each metric uses is shown beneath the description.

    Today's read (Home)

    Today's session

    Realized P/L from trades you closed today, plus the count.

    Σ pl(t) where exit_date == today

    Slots open

    How many more positions you can open before hitting your max open positions limit.

    maxPositions - openPositions

    Sector lean

    Top-rated sectors (Strong Buy / Buy) you should bias toward; Avoid list is sectors graded Sell or Strong Sell. Update on Sundays.

    Last 30-day P/L

    Realized P/L over the rolling window you set in Settings. The kill switch fires when this number is worse than -7% of your account.

    Σ pl(t) where exit_date more than today - killSwitchDays

    Career read (Stats)

    Total P/L

    Realized profit or loss across every closed trade in the current filter. Open trades counted separately as Open exposure.

    Σ pl(t) where status != 'open'

    Win rate

    Percentage of closed trades with positive P/L. Doesn't say anything about size of wins or losses — pair with Avg R.

    wins / (wins + losses) × 100

    Avg R / Trade

    Average outcome in units of risk. +1R means the trade made one times the planned risk dollars. +0.5R averaged across many trades is a strong edge.

    R(t) = pl(t) / riskDollars(t)
    avg = mean(R) over closed trades

    R Expectancy

    What an average trade should make you in R, weighted by win rate. Above 0R = profitable system.

    winRate × avgWinR + lossRate × avgLossR

    Profit Factor

    Total dollars won divided by total dollars lost. 1.50+ is good; 2.00+ is rare. Above 1.00 means winners outpace losers in dollar terms.

    Σ wins / |Σ losses|

    Expectancy ($)

    Average dollar result per closed trade. Multiplies trade volume to project monthly P/L.

    Σ pl(t) / count(closed)

    Open exposure

    Estimated dollars at risk across currently-open positions, summed from each trade's logged risk dollars.

    Σ riskDollars(t) where status == 'open'

    Best / Worst setup

    Pattern with the highest / lowest cumulative P/L across all closed trades. Needs more than 5 closed trades for the comparison to be meaningful.

    Execution intelligence

    Stock vs options edge

    Compares closed stock trades against closed option trades using P/L, win rate, and average R.

    group by instrument; avgR = mean(pl / riskDollars)

    Spread drag

    Splits intraday option trades by bid/ask spread. Wide spreads show whether market-maker friction is eating the setup.

    spread% = (ask - bid) / mid × 100

    Fill quality

    Compares your entry to the option mid. Paying above mid is execution drag; getting mid or better is clean.

    slippage% = (entry - mid) / mid × 100

    Intraday context intelligence

    Context alignment

    Scores whether setup bias, VWAP confluence, and breadth agree with the trade direction.

    alignment = passing context checks / available checks

    ORB quality

    Separates ORB up/down trades and OR range buckets, then tracks P/L, win rate, avg R, and target-hit rate.

    OR range% = RNG / OR_HI × 100

    VWAP edge

    Groups intraday trades by LONG BIAS, SHORT BIAS, MIXED, or legacy above/below VWAP labels.

    Execution friction score

    Blends spread, fill quality, entry window, context alignment, and trade number of day into Low / Moderate / High friction buckets.

    score = average(pass/fail execution checks) × 100

    Process quality

    Process Quality (Good %)

    How often you followed the plan. Each closed trade is graded Good (followed setup, sizing, exit rules), Okay (one minor drift), or Bad (broke multiple rules).

    Good count / graded count × 100

    Discretionary / thesis-broke exits

    Trades exited on a gut call or because the thesis broke before reaching target or stop. Often a behavioral leak — these tend to net negative.

    Risk controls

    Kill switch

    Smart-Stop trigger. Fires when rolling P/L over your kill-switch window is worse than -7% of account. While active, the new-trade flow is blocked. Window is editable in Settings (default 30 days).

    kill = (Σ pl over last N days) / account ≤ -7%

    Risk per trade (regime-based)

    How much of the account is risked on a single trade. Risk-on uses the high tier, Neutral the mid, Risk-off the low. Set in Settings.

    nextRisk = account × regimeRiskPct

    Max open positions

    The only portfolio-wide gate. It caps simultaneously-open trades across swing and intraday.
